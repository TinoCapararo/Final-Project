Discover the Connection Between Emotions and Environments

The Emotional Interaction Board is an immersive experience designed to help users explore the deep relationship between emotions and different environments. By interacting with the board, you can visualize how your feelings—whether it's calm, anxiety, sadness, or hope—connect with various surroundings.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Pick an Emotion

    Choose an emotion card that represents your current emotional state.

  • Step 2: Select a Scenario

    Select a scenario card that resonates with your chosen emotion.

  • Step 3: Reflect

    Observe how the combination of emotion and scenario makes you feel.
